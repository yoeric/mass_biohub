## BioFoundry {#biofoundry}
**What it supports**
- Design-build-test-learn workflows
- High-throughput strain construction and screening
- Standardized metadata and experiment tracking (as applicable)

**Capabilities (examples)**
- DNA assembly and cloning workflows
- Automated liquid handling
- Screening assays (growth, production, activity)
- Sample prep and analytics pipelines

**Engagement**
- Shared access with training
- Collaborative workflow development
- Sponsored projects for rapid prototyping

<hr/>

## Scale-Up {#scaleup}
**What it supports**
- Process development and optimization
- Scale-down models for predictive scale-up
- Tech transfer readiness and reproducibility

**Capabilities (examples)**
- Bench-scale bioreactors (batch/fed-batch) as available
- Process monitoring and control
- Downstream processing exploration (as applicable)
- Analytics and QA/QC-aligned documentation templates

**Engagement**
- Milestone-based development programs
- Joint scale-up planning and risk reduction
- Data packages for decision-making

<hr/>

## Builder’s Lab {#builderslab}
**What it supports**
- Prototyping of lab hardware and instrumentation
- Sensors, automation enclosures, fixtures
- Training: safety, practical tools, and build literacy

**Capabilities (examples)**
- Basic fabrication and assembly
- Electronics prototyping (as applicable)
- Instrument integration and test rigs
- Documentation-first build practices

**Engagement**
- Workshops and short courses
- Partner-supported build projects
- Student and community maker collaborations
